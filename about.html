Digital Designer with a focus on Front-End Web Developement

Shaun Stone was born in California, and grew up in a very small town called Placerville. During is Freshman year of high school, he started to develop websites and fell in love right away. After high school, he joined the United States Army and served 7 years honorably as an Infantryman.

After leaving the military in 2013, Shaun started to attend the University of Maryland to receive is Bachelors Degree in Digital Media and Web Technology. He started using online resources to catch up with how much web design had changed during his 7 year hiatus, and has mostly self taught himself many different languages, frameworks, and database managment.
